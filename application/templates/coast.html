Mombasa Value Tour

A Mombasa tour offers a perfect blend of sun-kissed beaches, rich Swahili culture, and historic landmarks for an unforgettable coastal escape.

Book Now

Day 1: Nairobi to Mombasa – Exploring the Old Town • Morning: o Depart Nairobi early (6:00 AM). o Arrive in Mombasa by early afternoon (around 2:00 PM). o Check in to a budget guesthouse or hotel in the city center. • Afternoon: o After lunch (own cost), head to Mombasa Old Town for a walking tour. Explore the narrow streets, visit Fort Jesus, and admire the Swahili architecture. • Evening: o Enjoy a relaxed dinner at a local restaurant. o Take a stroll along Mombasa’s Waterfront, enjoying the evening breeze. ________________________________________ Day 2: Mombasa Beaches & Haller Park • Morning: o After breakfast, head to Nyali Beach or Diani Beach for some sun, sea, and sand. Enjoy swimming or just relax by the beach (beach activities at own cost). • Afternoon: o Visit Haller Park for a guided walk and to see wildlife like giraffes, buffaloes, and tortoises. o Have a light lunch within the park (own cost). • Evening: o Head back to the hotel to freshen up. o For dinner, try local seafood dishes at a budget-friendly seaside restaurant. ________________________________________ Day 3: Mombasa City & Return to Nairobi • Morning: o After breakfast, visit Mamba Village (crocodile farm) to learn about crocodiles and see other animals (entrance fees: approximately Ksh 500). o Visit Shree Cutch Satsang Swaminarayan Temple, a peaceful and beautiful place of worship with vibrant murals and architecture. • Afternoon: o Enjoy a simple lunch before heading back to the bus station for your return trip to Nairobi. o Depart Mombasa by 2:00 PM, arriving back in Nairobi by evening.

A Classic Diani Getaway

A Diani tour promises pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and luxurious coastal vibes, perfect for relaxation and adventure.

Book Now

4-Day Diani Tour Itinerary Day 1: Nairobi to Diani – Beach Relaxation • Morning: o Depart Nairobi early (6:00 AM) via bus or shuttle to Diani. o Arrive in Diani by late afternoon and check in to a budget guesthouse or hotel near the beach. • Evening: o After settling in, enjoy a relaxed walk along the Diani Beach and take in the beautiful coastal scenery. o Enjoy a simple dinner at a beachside restaurant (own cost). ________________________________________ Day 2: Beach Day & Water Sports • Morning: o After breakfast, spend the morning lounging on Diani Beach. Swim, sunbathe, or take a relaxing walk along the shore. • Afternoon: o Try out affordable water activities such as snorkeling, kitesurfing, or a glass-bottom boat ride (prices may vary, but there are budget-friendly options). o Enjoy a simple lunch at a beach restaurant. • Evening: o After some relaxation, have dinner at a local restaurant by the beach. ________________________________________ Day 3: Shimba Hills National Reserve & Mwaluganje Elephant Sanctuary • Morning: o After breakfast, head to Shimba Hills National Reserve for a game drive. Spot elephants, giraffes, and buffaloes in this beautiful forest reserve. (Park entry fees: Ksh 600 for residents). • Afternoon: o Continue your adventure with a visit to Mwaluganje Elephant Sanctuary to learn about elephant conservation efforts. o Enjoy a packed lunch in the park or at a nearby local eatery. • Evening: o Return to Diani in the late afternoon. Relax by the beach before enjoying dinner at a nearby restaurant. ________________________________________ Day 4: Diani & Return to Nairobi • Morning: o After breakfast, take a last stroll along Diani Beach or visit the Colobus Conservation Centre to learn about the local monkeys (entrance fees: Ksh 500). • Afternoon: o Have a simple lunch before heading to the bus station for the return trip to Nairobi. o Depart Diani in the afternoon and arrive in Nairobi by evening.

Visit local shops, markets, and the Malindi Museum. • Evening: o Enjoy a relaxed dinner at the hotel or at a seaside restaurant, offering fresh seafood. ________________________________________ Day 2: Malindi Marine National Park & Watamu Beach • Morning: o After breakfast, head to Malindi Marine National Park for a morning of snorkeling, glass-bottom boat rides, or dolphin watching (budget-friendly options are available). • Afternoon: o Have a packed or simple lunch. o Spend the afternoon at Watamu Beach, a pristine and quiet location ideal for swimming and relaxing. • Evening: o Return to Malindi for dinner, either at the hotel or a beachside restaurant. ________________________________________ Day 3: Gede Ruins & Marafa Depression (Hell’s Kitchen) • Morning: o Breakfast at the hotel. o Head to the Gede Ruins for a cultural exploration of this historical Swahili town, dating back to the 12th century (entrance fee: Ksh 500). • Afternoon: o After exploring the ruins, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. o In the afternoon, visit Marafa Depression (also known as Hell's Kitchen) to see the beautiful, colorful rock formations. • Evening: o Return to the hotel for a relaxed dinner and unwind. ________________________________________ Day 4: Bio-Ken Snake Farm & Malindi Town Exploration • Morning: o After breakfast, visit the Bio-Ken Snake Farm to learn about Kenya’s snake species, conservation efforts, and interact with various reptiles (entrance fee: Ksh 500). • Afternoon: o Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant in Malindi town. o Spend the afternoon at the Malindi Mosque and the nearby Portuguese Chapel, exploring Malindi's rich history. • Evening: o Return to the hotel for dinner and enjoy an evening stroll by the beach. ________________________________________ Day 5: Malindi Town & Return to Nairobi • Morning: o Breakfast and check out of the hotel. o Visit Arabuko Sokoke Forest (optional, entry fee: Ksh 200) for a nature walk or birdwatching. • Afternoon: o Have a simple lunch before beginning the journey back to Nairobi. o Depart Malindi by 2:00 PM and arrive in Nairobi by evening.
